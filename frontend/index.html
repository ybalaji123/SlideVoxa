Support for .ppt and .pptx files. We extract titles, bullet points, and all text content automatically.

AI Script Generation

Sarvam AI converts each slide's content into a natural, engaging speaking script — just like a real presenter.

AI Voice Narration

Natural-sounding AI voices powered by ElevenLabs. Upload your own voice sample for a personal touch.

Auto Presentation Player

Slides advance automatically as narration plays. Full controls: prev, next, pause, and volume slider.
